How It Works

From zero to applied
in under 5 minutes

01
Upload Your Resume
Drop in your PDF. LYNK's NLP engine parses your skills, job titles, education, and experience automatically.
02
Set Your Preferences
Pick your target roles, industry, location, salary range, and whether you want remote, hybrid, or on-site work.
03
Swipe Through Matches
Your personalised feed surfaces the highest-match jobs first. Swipe right to apply, left to skip, up to save.
04
Auto-Apply
On a right swipe, LYNK auto-fills or redirects your application on supported job boards instantly.
05
Track Everything
Watch your applications move from Applied → Interviewing → Offer in one clean dashboard.
